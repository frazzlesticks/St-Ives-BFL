A 2 team, annual Beach Football League held on Porthminster Beach in St. Ives between the Sharks and the reigning champions, the Seagulls.

Our games are 3 a side, and both teams have a fourth player who joins in a few times each season for 4-a-side games. They can also replace injured players from the original 3.
